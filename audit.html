Executive Summary

This preliminary assessment of BNBNest, a pre-launch DeFi protocol, was conducted based on available documentation. The core risk is the absence of a formal third-party audit for its smart contracts. BNBNexa

Whitepaper & Tokenomics Review

Date

Oct 2025

Version

1.0 (Pre-launch)

Chain

BSC

Risk Score

Medium

Executive Summary

Our review of BNBNexa's pre-launch materials indicates a standard multi-pool staking protocol design. The primary risk factor is the centralization of control with an anonymous team and the lack of an external code audit. The tokenomics model proposes a rewards system that may be inflationary if not managed with care through utility and burn mechanisms. Proceeding without a formal audit is not recommended.

High Lack of Third-Party Smart Contract Audit Pending

BNB Capital

Full Smart Contract Audit

Date

Sep 2025

Version

2.1 (Live)

Chain

BSC

Risk Score

Medium

Executive Summary

The audit of BNB Capital's live smart contracts identified one medium-severity and two informational findings. The core logic for yield aggregation is sound, but the medium-severity finding points to a potential precision loss issue in reward calculation under specific edge cases. The team has acknowledged the finding and plans a fix in the next upgrade. Systemic risk from dependency on external protocols remains the primary operational concern.

Vulnerability Findings

SeverityFindingStatus
Medium Precision Loss in Reward Calculation Acknowledged
Info Gas Optimization Opportunities Mitigated
