About DeFi Portal

Discover how we're transforming DeFi discovery with transparent scoring and community-driven insights

What is DeFi Portal?

DeFi Portal is a curated portal that highlights decentralized finance projects after a structured review, helping users discover opportunities aligned with their risk tolerance.

Mission

To raise the standard of due diligence in DeFi by publishing transparent criteria, consistent scoring, and concise summaries anyone can understand.

Why We Exist

DeFi moves fast and information is fragmented. We centralize key signals—security, liquidity, team, and tokenomics—into a digestible format.

How We Curate

Projects are shortlisted through open-source intelligence, on-chain data, and third-party audit references before being added to our watchlist.

Transparent Scoring

Each listing displays a risk tier and context notes. Our scoring rubric is public and continuously refined with community feedback.

Independent & Educational

We do not offer financial advice or paid promotion. Our content is educational and designed to support individual decision-making.

Our Methodology

Our rigorous evaluation process ensures every project meets high standards before being featured

Security & Audits

We check for formal audits, open-source contracts, time-locks, and multi-sig controls, and we review known vulnerabilities for the protocol class.

Team & Governance

We assess team transparency, track record, governance mechanisms, and response quality to disclosures or incidents.

Tokenomics & Incentives

We analyze emissions, utility, vesting schedules, treasury design, and sustainability of incentives over time.

Liquidity & Integrations

We review liquidity depth, chain support, oracle design, key integrations, and composability risks.

On-Chain Health

We monitor TVL trends, user activity, address distribution, and anomalous patterns using on-chain analytics.

Community Trust

We evaluate community engagement, social sentiment, and responsiveness to user concerns and feedback.
