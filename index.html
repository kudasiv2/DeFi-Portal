About DeFi Portal

What is DeFi Portal?

A curated platform that highlights decentralized finance projects after comprehensive review, helping users discover opportunities aligned with their risk tolerance.

Our Mission

To elevate due diligence standards in DeFi through transparent criteria, consistent scoring, and accessible summaries for all experience levels.

Why We Exist

DeFi moves rapidly with fragmented information. We centralize critical signals—security, liquidity, team, and tokenomics—into digestible formats.

How We Curate

Projects undergo rigorous screening using open-source intelligence, on-chain analytics, and third-party audit verification before inclusion.

Transparent Scoring

Each listing displays a risk tier with detailed context. Our public scoring framework evolves with community feedback and market developments.

Independent & Educational

We provide educational content without financial advice or paid promotions, supporting informed individual decision-making.

Our Methodology

Security & Audits

We verify formal audits, open-source contracts, time-locks, multi-sig controls, and assess known vulnerabilities for each protocol category.

Team & Governance

We evaluate team transparency, track records, governance mechanisms, and response quality to disclosures or incidents.

Tokenomics & Incentives

We analyze emissions, utility, vesting schedules, treasury design, and long-term incentive sustainability.

Liquidity & Integrations

We review liquidity depth, chain support, oracle design, key integrations, and composability risks.

On-Chain Health

We monitor TVL trends, user activity, address distribution, and anomalous patterns using advanced on-chain analytics.

Community Engagement

We assess community size, engagement quality, developer activity, and responsiveness to community feedback.
