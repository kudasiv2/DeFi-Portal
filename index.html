Our Audit Methodology

1. Smart Contract Analysis

Line-by-line code review to detect common and novel vulnerabilities, including re-entrancy, integer overflows, and access control issues.

2. Economic Model Assessment

Stress-testing tokenomics, incentive mechanisms, and oracle dependencies to identify potential vectors for economic manipulation.

3. On-Chain Forensics

Analysis of wallet distributions, governance control, and protocol health metrics to uncover centralization risks and signs of malicious intent.

4. Comprehensive Reporting

Delivering a detailed audit report with actionable recommendations, severity ratings, and mitigation status for each identified finding.
