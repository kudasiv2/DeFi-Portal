Our Methodology

Security & Audits

We check for formal audits, open-source contracts, time-locks, and multi-sig controls, and we review known vulnerabilities for the protocol class.

Team & Governance

We assess team transparency, track record, governance mechanisms, and response quality to disclosures or incidents.

Tokenomics & Incentives

We analyze emissions, utility, vesting schedules, treasury design, and sustainability of incentives over time.

Liquidity & Integrations

We review liquidity depth, chain support, oracle design, key integrations, and composability risks.

On-Chain Health

We monitor TVL trends, user activity, address distribution, and anomalous patterns using on-chain analytics.

DeFi Guides

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) replaces intermediaries with smart contracts to enable open, permissionless financial services.

Start with audits and code openness, then assess governance, tokenomics, liquidity, and historical incident response.

Total Value Locked (TVL) measures assets deposited in a protocol. It shows adoption but not safety by itself.

Use hardware or reputable wallets, enable 2FA where possible, verify contract addresses, and keep seed phrases offline.

Audits reduce but do not eliminate risk. Look for the scope, severity breakdown, and remediation status.

Beware of unrealistic yields, opaque teams, unaudited contracts, and permissions that allow fund movement.

Bridge only what you can afford to risk, prefer audited bridges with bug bounty programs, and confirm destination addresses.
